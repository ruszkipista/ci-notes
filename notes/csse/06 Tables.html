Tables

Styling tables is a bit more involved than working with fonts, text, lists, etc. There are so many ways to slice and dice the formatting of cells, rows, columns and the table itself. Here's a few examples to understand the IMPACT of various settings. A fully styled table is the domain of a talented designer.
